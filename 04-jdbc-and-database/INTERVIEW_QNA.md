## Q1. Why do databases exist?

To store, manage and query data efficiently.

---

## Q2. What problems do databases solve?

Duplication, inconsistency, poor querying and concurrency issues.

---

## Q3. What is data persistence?

Data survives application restart.

---

## Q4. Why are databases important in backend engineering?

Backend systems fundamentally depend on persistent data.

---

## Q5. What is structured data?

Data organized in predefined format.

---

## Q6. What are relational databases?

Databases organizing data into related tables.

---

## Q7. Why are relational databases important for Spring Boot?

Spring ecosystems heavily depend on relational persistence.

---

## Q8. What are transactional systems?

Systems requiring reliable consistent operations.

---

## Q9. Why is consistency important in backend systems?

Incorrect data may break business workflows.

---

## Q10. Difference between file storage and databases?

Databases provide structure, querying, consistency and concurrency handling.
